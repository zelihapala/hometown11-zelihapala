“The Pearl of the Aegean Sea”

Izmir is my hometown. Our home is preferable to all other homes that's why we say “Home sweet home!” wherever I live I always miss my city. Izmir an ancient city, with its beautiful districts and deep-rooted historical heritage, with many places to visit. The city is very popular for both domestic and foreign tourists in terms of tourism. Turkish people call this city as "the pearl of the Aegean".

Izmir has a lot to see such as cultural center with palm-fringed boulevards, graced by elegant mosques and traditional bazaars, and ancient Greek ruins peering down from the hills. Kemeraltı is one of the world's oldest bazaars. There are approximately 2 thousand registered buildings in the bazaar. It is also a region where all three religions have lived in harmony with its churches, mosques and synagogues.

If you are ready, let's start our list of places to visit in Izmir!

Izmir Agora, Ephesus, Pergamon Acropolis, Kemeralti Market, Mount Kadifekale , Kordon, Asansör, ?zmir ClockTower, Izmir Museum of History & Art, Konak are the most populer places.

Don’t forget to try Izmir’s cuisine as well.

İzmir has a wide variety in terms of street tastes. You can find İzmir’s “gevrek, boyoz and simit” in every corner of the city and these are indispensable for breakfasts.Never come back before trying cold cut meat, shish kebab and sherbet that cools you in summer.
